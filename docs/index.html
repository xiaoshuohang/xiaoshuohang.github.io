About this application

Play with the application below by adjusting the range of latitude and longitude on the 4 slider bars.This makes us delve into the investigation of the current conditions about the marine species counts.

This app is a representation of a dataset provided by the Aqua Map, which includes the computer-generated predictions of the natural distribution of marine species. These predictions are derived from the environmental preferences of each species, considering factors like depth, temperature,and their correlation with sea ice or coastal regions.This map primarily represents the marine species count within a distance of 50 km near the equator,a total of 33,512 species were used in the generation of this file.
